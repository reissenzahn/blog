--- title: "Carbonara" date: 2021-02-22 draft: false ---

Original Recipe

Ingredients

For one serving:

  • 30g pecorino romano cheese
  • 100g spaghetti
  • 1 egg
  • guanciale, pancetta or bacon
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Add grated pecorino roman, egg and black pepper to bowl and whisk until smooth.
  2. Dice bacon and add cook in a pan with olive oil.
  3. Take pan off heat to cool down once done.
  4. Cook pasta until just before al dente.
  5. Add pasta to pan with a little pasta water.
  6. Agitate vigorously while adding egg mixture until appropriately thickened.
