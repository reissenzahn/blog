Original Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g dry spaghetti
  • 12 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 30g pecorino romano cheese
  • scallions

Method

  1. Grate pecorino romano and thinly slice scallions.
  2. Crush garlic with the flat side of a knife and mash it in a mortar and pestle along with a pinch of salt.
  3. Cook pasta according to package instructions in unsalted water.
  4. Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Add the garlic and cook stirring until fragrant but not browned (about 2 minutes).
  6. Add oyster sauce, soy sauce and fish sauce and stir to combine.
  7. Transfer cooked pasta to garlic sauce and increase heat to high.
  8. Add cheese and scallions and stir and toss vigorously until the sauce is creamy and emulsified (about 30 seconds).
  9. If sauce looks too watery, let it keep reducing; if it looks greasy, splash some more pasta water into it and let it re-emulsify.
