--- title: "Sweat and Sour Chicken" date: 2021-02-22 draft: false ---

Original Recipe

Ingredients

Meat:

  • 1 lb chicken thigh or breast

Marinade:

  • 1.5 tsp corn starch
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp Shaoxing wine
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 egg white

Vegetables:

  • 1 onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 10 oz can of pineapple

Aromatics:

  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 finger ginger

Sauce:

  • juice from 10 oz can of pineapple
  • 3 tbsp of ketchup
  • 2 tbsp of white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp plus 1/2 tsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp of soy sauce
  • 2 tsp of corn starch
  • splash of Shaoxing wine

Method

  1. Cut chicken into stir fry sized pieces and add to bowl with marinade ingredients.
  2. Mix vigorously and let stand for at least 15 mins.
  3. Large dice the onion, green, red bell peppers, and pineapple.
  4. Crush aromatics in mortar and pestle.
  5. Add sauce ingredients to bowl and whisk to combine.
  6. Bring 1 qt of water to a simmer in wok.
  7. Once simmering add chicken one piece at a time and cook for 30-60 seconds.
  8. Remove to sheet tray in a single layer to dry.
  9. Clean wok and dry the wok and place it back on the heat.
  10. Add a tiny amount of oil and rub into wok, once smoking the wok is preheated.
  11. Add a couple of tablespoons of oil and swirl to coat wok.
  12. Add onion and the green and red bell peppers, until tender crisp.
  13. Push to one side, add a little more oil and add aromatics.
  14. Mix and let stir fry for about 30 seconds.
  15. Add chicken, pineapple, some cashews and the sauce.
  16. Let cook a little bit until desired thickness is reached.
