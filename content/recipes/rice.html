Original Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • pinch of salt
  • 2-3 star aniseed

Method

  1. Rise rice to remove extra starch.
  2. Add rice and water to pot.
  3. Add pinch of salt and star aniseed.
  4. Bring to boil.
  5. Put lid on pot and set temperature to the lowest setting.
  6. Simmer for 18 minutes.
