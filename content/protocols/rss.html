--- title: "Really Simple Syndication" date: 2021-12-29 draft: false ---
  • RSS is a web feed that provides access to updates to websites in a standardized machine-readable format.
  • Users subscribe to RSS feeds using a news aggregator which monitors the sites for new content and displays it in one place.
  • Websites usually use RSS feeds to publish frequently updated information such as blog entries, news headlines, episodes of audio and video series or for distributing podcasts.
